About

    

If you're someone looking to help your community by providing greatly-needed services/products during COVID-19, or you're a relief organization hoping to meet your needs, look no further.

    

Prospective Volunteer: Simply make an account, and search up orgs or requests that have been made by others in your area. If you find something you can commit, chat with the org!

    

Organization: Simply make an account, and create descriptive requests that detail what you need to succeed! Chat with volunteers who can fulfill your needs.
